Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free to start? ▼

Yes! Sign up and instantly get 100 free verification credits — no credit card required. Use them for single email checks or small bulk lists. When you run out, you can contact us to upgrade to a paid plan.

What is a "credit" and how does it work? ▼

1 credit = 1 email verified. When you verify a single email, 1 credit is deducted. When you bulk-verify 500 emails, 500 credits are deducted. Credits do not expire and roll over month to month.

Can I verify Gmail, Yahoo and Outlook emails? ▼

These providers (Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook, Hotmail) block SMTP probing to protect user privacy — this is an industry-wide limitation, not specific to Veridrax. For these domains we return a CATCH-ALL status, meaning the domain is real and receives mail, but individual mailbox existence cannot be confirmed via SMTP. All other corporate and business emails are fully verified.

How do I verify a large list (bulk)? ▼

After logging in, go to your Dashboard → Bulk Verify tab. Upload a .csv or .txt file with one email per line. The system will process them and let you download a full results CSV when done. Bulk verification requires a Starter plan or higher.

Is my data safe? Do you store my email lists? ▼

We do not sell or share your data. Email addresses are processed in-memory for verification and result logs are stored in your private account only. All connections use TLS encryption. You can delete your history at any time from your dashboard.

Can I use the API to verify emails in my own app? ▼

Yes! All plans include API access. Go to Dashboard → API Keys to get your key, then send a POST request to /api/verify with your email in the request body and your API key in the X-API-Key header.

Privacy Policy & GDPR Compliance

Effective Date: April 2026. Data Security is our paramount concern.

1. Data Collection & Processing

When you use Veridrax to validate emails, we execute DNS lookups and SMTP connections. The email addresses uploaded via CSV or API are processed fleetingly in-memory on our highly secure, distributed workers. We do not persistently store, resell, or analyze your raw leads. Uploaded lists are deleted automatically post-processing as per our aggressive data retention protocol.

2. Security Architecture

All connections between your client and our API Gateway are heavily encrypted utilizing TLS 1.3. Our database arrays operate on isolated private subnets, shielded from lateral traversal via stateless VPC security group architectures. Authentication handles are tokenized securely without storing plaintext passwords.

3. Infrastructure & Third Parties

We do not share your billing or analytics data with third-party marketing trackers. Our physical proxy instances interact directly with the targeted mail servers solely for the explicit purpose of validation. We function purely as a technical conduit, affirming email deliverability.

4. Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA regulations, you harbor the right to be forgotten. You can purge your entire history and associated uploaded artifacts by contacting the administration via the Contact portal. Data purges execute irrevocably across our master databases and edge Redis clusters.
